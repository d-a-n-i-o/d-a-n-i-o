Hi! I’m Kayla—an interaction designer-in-training who believes every project deserves its own distinct and intuitive visual language.

I believe that humans should always be front and center in design, every interaction should feel intuitive, thoughtful, and meaningful. My approach blends creativity, curiosity, and attention to detail, whether I’m designing interfaces, refining user flows, or exploring unique layouts.

With a background in e-commerce support at Shopify, I’ve seen firsthand how thoughtful design improves real interactions, shaping my approach to creating seamless and engaging experiences. Beyond visuals, I value one-on-one communication within teams, ensuring that collaboration is as intentional as the experiences we create.

When I’m not designing, you’ll find me cooking up a storm, watching a heavy dose of reality TV, or hanging out with Pinot Noir—the cutest forever kitten in the world 🐈‍⬛💖.
